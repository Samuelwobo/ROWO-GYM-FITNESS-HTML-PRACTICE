WHO WE ARE

ROWO-FITNESS GYM & WELLNESS CENTRE

ROWO-Fitness is a health and wellness company. With 21 branches across Nigeria, our highly equipped gym facilities complemented by the expertise of over 250 professionally certified personal trainers provide our members with a holistic and all-inclusive fitness and wellness experience. Our offerings include multi-location access and over 50 varied monthly fitness programs to suit and support the fitness lifestyles of our vast membership base. ROWO-Fitness is committed to providing a youthful, entertaining yet rewarding fitness experience to many thousands of people, thereby promoting access to happier and healthier lives.


i-Fitness is managed by a Board of Directors comprising seasoned professionals with extensive years of experience and a range of diverse specialities that complement the growth and development of the company.

Foluso Ogunwale

Foluso is the founder and CEO of ROWO-Fitness Gym and Wellness Centre Ltd. He is a serial entrepreneur with several years of experience in Sales, Marketing and HR with Skye Bank Plc. He began his journey into entrepreneurship in 2013. He founded Premier Impact Ltd in 2011, which later became Yes Mobile in 2015 – an electronics retail chain with 5 branches in Lagos. The company was the first to launch two innovative retail initiatives in Nigeria in mobile: the mobile electronics insurance with Axa Mansard PLC and the mobile swap initiative enabling customers to trade in their old devices (laptops & phones) for new ones. He successfully led the company from an annual turnover of N400m in 2011 to N4bn annual turnover in 2016 before entering into an acquisition deal with Yudala Nigeria Ltd, now Konga Ltd. Foluso founded i-Fitness in 2015 with the vision to build Africa’s preferred fitness chain. The company is on a mission to make the culture of health and fitness common, more fun, more convenient and more affordable to millions of Nigerians and Africans. Foluso has a B.Sc in Accounting and MSc. Financial Economics from the University of London, and is a senior professional in Human Resources (SHRM/SPHR).
